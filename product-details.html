Requirements:

  • Setup Area:

    We can set up on turf, grass, carpet, concrete and asphalt. We will not set up on dirt, rocks, gravel, sand or mulched areas. Setup will not be conducted on sloping terrain, or rooftop. Area must be cleared, clean and dry prior to our staff arriving.

  • Power:

    Our Inflatables are inflated using a fully enclosed electric blower unit that is powered by a standard 110 volt, 15 AMP, three-prong electrical outlet. Each blower will need to be on a separate circuit and cannot be shared with any other units or vendors.

  • Access:

    Clear and unobstructed access is crucial for a smooth setup process. Ensure that there is a direct path from where our truck parks to the designated setup area. If stairs or an elevator are required to reach the setup location, please notify us in advance to make necessary arrangements.

  • Gated Communities, Buildings and Venues:

    Many locations restrict commercial trucks during weekends or have limited hours. Confirm that our 17ft Box Truck can enter the premises. We cannot be held responsible if access is denied and unable to set up. In the event we cannot enter the premises for pickup, an additional day's charge will be incurred for each day the equipment remains until retrieval, along with a $250 inconvenience fee.

Safety Rules

We are insured and SIOTO Certified.

Safety Rules:

  • An adult must supervise at all times.
  • No shoes, eyeglasses, jewelry, or sharp objects allowed on the unit.
  • No food or drinks allowed.
  • Avoid face/body paint, slime, silly string, glitter, or any stains.
  • Keep pets away.
  • Avoid the inflatable if pregnant or with medical conditions.
  • No use during winds over 15 mph, thunderstorms, or lightning.
  • No wrestling, tumbling, flipping, climbing, or pulling.
  • Keep kids away from the back and blower.
  • Enter only if an attendant is on duty.
  • No rough play, tumbling, climbing, or piling on
  • Match adults with adults and children with children
  • If using slides, sit with feet in front; no headfirst sliding; one rider at a time.
  • Don't move the inflatable from the original location.
